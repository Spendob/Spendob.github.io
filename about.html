About Me

I'm a 21-year-old Florida native and Multiplatform Journalism student at the University of Maryland.

I've always been interested in storytelling, and found the best way to do it at a young age was to create short films with my friends. That blossomed into my interest in news and visually telling non-fiction stories as well.

Since coming to UMD, I have expanded my storytelling to writing, photography and videography, as well as the related skills. I'm currently working as an Editorial intern at PressBox Media, writing and editing Baltimore sports stories.
